    About Department

    The Department of Philosophy and Social Sciences (Head of the Department - Doctor of Political Sciences, Professor N. A. Latigin) was established in 1966. In the 2016/2017 academic year, the department provides teaching of 28 disciplines, including normative humanitarian disciplines; discipline on the choice of students; discipline for postgraduate students and graduate students. To date, educational and methodological support for all disciplines has been created. Developed programs and work programs, reference notes of lectures, computer programs for student knowledge control, methodical recommendations for independent work of full-time and part-time students; published textbooks, textbooks, textbooks, workshops, etc.
